# Production Access Policy — Emergency Revert

The self-service IAM portal introduced in May 2026 has been suspended following a
security incident. Effective immediately, all production access requests must go
through the original SRE pairing process.

## Reinstated process

1. Post in `#sre-access-requests` on Slack.
2. Tag the on-call SRE and your direct manager.
3. The on-call SRE will schedule a 30-minute pairing session within 2 working days.
4. Access is provisioned via Okta after the pairing session completes.

## Tenure requirement

Reverted to 2 weeks minimum. Engineers in their first 2 weeks should request an
urgent exception as before — the on-call SRE can grant 24-hour temporary access.

The BTS Learning portal course ("Prod Access Foundations") is suspended pending
an audit. Do not direct engineers there.
