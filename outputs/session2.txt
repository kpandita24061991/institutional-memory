## Getting read-only prod access as a new hire

### ⚠️ First: check your tenure

The minimum is **3 working days**. You can request access starting on **day 4**. If you joined yesterday or today and need access *tomorrow*, you may be blocked — see the escalation note at the bottom.

---

### The new self-serve process (effective 2026-05-15)

**Step 1 — Get your manager's sign-off on the certification**
Manager approval is required once, for the initial certification. Loop in your manager right away and ask them to approve you starting the "Prod Access Foundations" course.

**Step 2 — Complete the "Prod Access Foundations" course**
Go to the **BTS Learning portal** and take this course. It's self-paced, ~90 minutes. You'll need to pass the assessment at the end.

**Step 3 — Request access via the IAM platform**
The course completion email includes a link to the IAM platform. Submit your access request there. Access is granted **just-in-time in 4-hour windows** — so if you need it for a debugging session tomorrow, submit the request when you're ready to start.

Your manager will be notified automatically. No further sign-off is needed beyond the initial certification approval.

---

### People involved

| Who | Why |
|---|---|
| **Your direct manager** | Required for one-time certification approval; notified of each access request |
| Nobody else required | The process is fully self-serve via the Learning portal + IAM platform |

You do **not** need to contact SRE, open a Slack ticket, or schedule a pairing session.
