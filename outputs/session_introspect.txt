## Production Access Policy

### Current policy — effective 2026-05-15
*(Policy owners: Maya Singh + Carlos Mendes. Triggered by incident PROD-INC-04-2026. Supersedes January 2026 version.)*

Three tiers:
1. **Read-only**: 3 working days tenure + Prod Access Foundations cert (BTS Learning portal, ~90 min, self-paced). **No SRE pairing. No Slack ticket.** Access is JIT, scoped to a 4-hour window per request. Manager approves the initial cert once; auto-notified on subsequent requests.
